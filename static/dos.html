Denial of Service

Denial of Service (DOS) and Distributed Denial of Service (DDOS) is a hacking technique used to take down services uses use on the internet. It is done by flooding a webpage or webservice with so many requests that it fails. In doing so the service is denied to all users including real users.

In this exercise, your goal is to crash this very service by slowing down requests to the point that the webpage cannot respond within 30 seconds. The key to denial of service is that your computer must spend less time on a request than the server processing it.

Can your team figure out how to crash this service?

Goal: Crash this service (this page right here)!
